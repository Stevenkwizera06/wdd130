Our Featured Trips

Family Float

A gentle and scenic float down the calmer sections of the river. Perfect for families with young children or those looking for a relaxing day on the water exploring the beautiful ecosystem.

Difficulty: Beginner

Half-Day Thriller

Experience the excitement of Class III and IV rapids in this action-packed half-day adventure. This trip requires teamwork and is great for beginners looking for a thrill and intermediate rafters.

Difficulty: Intermediate

Extreme Expedition

A full-day journey through the most challenging and exhilarating rapids we offer. Prepare to be soaked as you navigate Class IV and V rapids. Previous experience is recommended for this trip.

Difficulty: Advanced
