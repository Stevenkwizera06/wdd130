Rapids Rush Rafting

At Rapids Rush Rafting, we are passionate about providing thrilling and unforgettable rafting experiences. Our team of expert guides is dedicated to ensuring your safety and enjoyment on the water. Whether you're a first-time rafter or a seasoned adventurer, we have the perfect trip waiting for you.

History

Rapids Rush Rafting was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who shared a love for white water adventures. Starting with just two rafts and a dream, our founders set out to share the exhilarating thrill of white water rafting with adventurers from all walks of life. Over the years, we have grown into one of the region's most trusted and beloved rafting companies, offering guided tours on some of the most spectacular rivers in the country. Our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and unforgettable experiences has earned us countless loyal customers and industry awards. Today, Rapids Rush Rafting proudly operates year-round with a fleet of modern equipment and a team of professionally certified guides ready to make your adventure dreams a reality.
