Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for rafting?

Wear quick-dry clothing, water shoes with straps, and bring a change of clothes. We provide life jackets and helmets.

Is rafting safe for children?

Yes! We offer family-friendly trips suitable for children 6 and older. All trips include safety equipment and certified guides.

What if the weather is bad?

We monitor weather conditions and may reschedule trips for safety. Full refunds are provided for weather-related cancellations.

Do I need experience to go rafting?

No experience needed! Our guides provide instruction and we offer trips for all skill levels, from beginners to experts.

Can I bring my own camera?

Yes, but we recommend waterproof cameras or cases. We also provide professional photos of your adventure.

What's included in the trip price?

All safety equipment, professional guides, transportation, meals (on longer trips), and photos are included in the price.
