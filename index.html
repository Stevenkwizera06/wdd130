Rwanda

Rwanda is a landlocked country in East Africa known as the "Land of a Thousand Hills." It's famous for its stunning mountain landscapes, including the Virunga Mountains, home to endangered mountain gorillas. Rwanda has made remarkable progress in economic development and environmental conservation, becoming one of Africa's cleanest and most organized countries. The capital city, Kigali, is a modern, vibrant city that showcases Rwanda's commitment to innovation and sustainable development.
